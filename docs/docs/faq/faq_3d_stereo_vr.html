Can I stream 3D video in HD?

In general, yes. But it depends if your hardware (especially the cpu) is performant enough to handle the encoding process. It can be an option to use the “nanocosmos H.264 Video Encoder QS” filter for hardware encoding.

Another factor to consider is the available bandwidth, because HD requires a higher bitrate >than lower resolution such as 320×240 or 640×480.

How do I start a 3D broadcast?

With the desktop application: nanocosmos Live Video Encoder

  • open the application
  • go to the input tab
  • check the checkbox “Video 2”
  • select the desired input devices and choose the resolution, etc.
  • choose the desired mix mode
  • press the “Start Encoding” button

Which stereoscopic modes does your 3D encoder support?

Following modes are available:

  • Side-by-side left/right
  • Side-by-side top/bottom
  • Interlaced lines
  • Interlaced columns
  • Anaglyph
